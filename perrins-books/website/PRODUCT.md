# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Anyone who wants to read the author's work. The context is a personal webpage focused on poetry and artistic expression.

## Product Purpose
A personal digital portfolio and publishing platform for the author's poetry. Success looks like an immersive, highly aesthetic reading experience that leaves a lasting impression on visitors.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, clean, wow-factor. The emotional goal is to impress the reader immediately while providing a quiet, elegant space to consume the writing.

## Anti-references
Generic templates, cluttered interfaces, overly complex navigation.

## Design Principles
- **Aesthetics First:** The design must deliver a "wow-factor" immediately upon load.
- **Content is Sacred:** The poetry must be effortlessly readable despite the bold surroundings.
- **Sleek Minimalism:** Keep the UI chrome clean and minimal to let the content and 3D backgrounds shine.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
No specific hard requirements (e.g., strict WCAG compliance is not requested), but fundamental readability against the dynamic backgrounds must be maintained.
